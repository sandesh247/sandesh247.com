== Description ==

Blogger to Wordpress allows you to redirect from your blogspot post to the corresponding wordpress post(Imported from blogger).

Why you need this plugin: If you are a blogspot user and now you have a wordpress blog(may be in your own domain), you can import all of your blogspot posts to your wordpress blog, but how you can redirect the visiters of your blogspot.com blog to the same post in your wordpress blog? There comes the Blogger to Wordpress plugin. Install and Activate the plugin, follow the simple 3 steps, you are done :)

== Installation ==

1. Unzip "wp-blogger-to-wordpress.zip" and upload the wp-blogger-to-wordpress directory into your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
the top-level folder. Don't just upload all the php files and put them in `/wp-content/plugins/`.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Visit your Blogger to Wordpress options to get your redirection script(or visit http://arunmvishnu.com/get-redirection-script ) and put it in your blogspot.com blog's template, You are done!
